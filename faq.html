Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pest control services and safety measures.

Are the chemicals safe for kids and pets?
Yes, safety is our top priority. We use government-approved, eco-friendly, and odorless chemicals that are specifically designed to be safe for children, pets, and pregnant women when dry. However, we do recommend keeping pets and kids away from the treated area for a few hours as a precaution.
How long does the treatment take?
The duration depends on the size of the property and the type of infestation. A general pest control treatment for a 2BHK apartment usually takes about 30 to 45 minutes. Termite treatments may take longer, often a few hours.
Do you provide a warranty?
Yes, we offer warranties on most of our services. Or annual maintenance contracts (AMC) come with a year-long guarantee where we provide free re-treatments if the problem persists. Specific warranty periods vary by service type.
How often should pest control be done?
For general maintenance, we recommend a quarterly service (every 3 months). However, for restaurants or areas with heavy infestations, monthly services might be required.
Is same-day service available?
Yes, we strive to offer same-day service for emergency requests booked before 12 PM. Availability may vary based on our technician's schedule for the day.
How do I book a service?
You can book a service by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form on our website. We will confirm the appointment time with you immediately.
