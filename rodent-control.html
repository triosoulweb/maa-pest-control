Rat Infestation Problems in West Gudur

Rats and mice are a common nuisance in Swarna Bharathi Nagar due to open fields and warehouses. They chew through electrical wires, potentially causing short circuits and fires. They also contaminate food and water sources with their urine and droppings, spreading diseases like Leptospirosis.

SG Pest Control offers comprehensive rodent management services for homes, shops, and factories.

Our Rodent Control Techniques

1. Rat Cakes (Rodenticides)

We place specialized bait stations containing slow-acting poison (rat cakes) in strategic locations outside the building. This controls the population effectively.

2. Glue Trap Stations

For indoor areas, especially kitchens and warehouses, we use strong glue pads that trap rats instantly. This is a non-toxic method safe for indoor use.

3. Entry Point Sealing

Our technicians identify holes and cracks where rats enter your property and advise on sealing them with concrete or wire mesh.

Prevention Tips for Gudur Residents

  • Keep garbage bins tightly sealed.
  • Do not leave pet food out overnight.
  • Store grains and food items in metal or glass containers.
  • Trim tree branches touching your roof or windows.
