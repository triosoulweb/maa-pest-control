Ant Infestation: A Persistent Nuisance

Ants are social insects that live in large colonies. In West Gudur Rural, we commonly encounter several species that invade homes in search of food and water. While some are just a nuisance, others can cause painful bites or damage wood.

DIY solutions like chalks and sprays often only kill the ants you see. They fail to eliminate the queen and the colony hidden deep within walls or underground. SG Pest Control provides a comprehensive solution to wipe out the entire colony.

Common Types of Ants in Gudur

Red Fire Ants

Known for their aggressive behavior and painful stings. They often build mounds in gardens and can enter homes.

Black Garden Ants

Often found marching towards sugary foods in kitchens ("Sugar Ants"). They contaminate food supplies.

Carpenter Ants

These large ants excavate wood to build nests, causing structural damage similar to termites.

Why Ants Are Dangerous

  • Food Contamination: Ants carry bacteria on their bodies and can contaminate open food in your kitchen.
  • Painful Bites: Red ant bites can cause severe irritation, swelling, and allergic reactions in some people.
  • Property Damage: Carpenter ants damage wooden furniture, doors, and window frames.
  • Electrical Damage: In rare cases, ants can nest inside electrical appliances causing short circuits.

Prevention Tips

  • Seal cracks and crevices in walls and foundations.
  • Keep the kitchen counter clean and free of crumbs.
  • Store sugar and sweet foods in airtight containers.
  • Trim vegetation touching the house to prevent ant bridges.
