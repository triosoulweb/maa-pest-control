The Importance of Mosquito Control

West Gudur Rural often faces severe mosquito problems due to stagnant water in paddy fields and open drains. Mosquitoes are the deadliest animals on earth, spreading fatal diseases like Malaria, Dengue fever, Chikungunya, and Zika virus.

During the monsoon season, mosquito breeding skyrockets. SG Pest Control offers timely interventions to reduce the adult mosquito population and kill larvae at the source.

Our Mosquito Management Services

1. Fogging (Thermal Fogging)

Ideal for large outdoor areas, gardens, and community compounds in Swarna Bharathi Nagar. It creates a dense fog that kills flying mosquitoes instantly.

2. Larvicidal Treatment

We identify breeding spots like stagnant water, drains, and coolers. We apply safe biological larvicides to kill mosquito larvae before they become adults.

3. Cold Fogging (ULV)

For indoor areas like bedrooms and living rooms. This water-based mist is odorless and safe for indoor use, leaving no oily residue.

Mosquito Prevention Checklist

  • Change water in coolers weekly.
  • Clear clogged roof gutters.
  • Cover water tanks tightly.
  • Use mosquito nets on windows.
  • Keep flower pot trays dry.
  • Close windows at dusk (5 PM - 7 PM).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is fogging safe for humans?

Outdoor thermal fogging uses diesel as a carrier, so avoid inhaling the smoke directly. Indoor cold fogging is water-based and much safer.

How long does the effect last?

Fogging kills present mosquitoes immediately but doesn't have a long residual effect. Larvicidal treatment prevents breeding for weeks. Regular service is recommended.

Do you serve independent houses?

Yes, we provide fogging for independent houses, apartments, and open plots in Gudur.
