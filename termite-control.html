Why Termite Control is Essential in Gudur

Termites (white ants) are a major problem in West Gudur Rural due to the soil conditions. They can silently destroy wooden furniture, doors, windows, and even structural foundations. SG Pest Control offers specialized anti-termite treatments designed for Indian construction types.

Signs of Termite Infestation

  • Mud tubes on walls or foundations.
  • Hollow-sounding wood when tapped.
  • Discarded wings near windows.
  • Fine sawdust (frass) near wooden items.

Our Treatment Methods

1. Pre-Construction Anti-Termite Treatment

Ideal for new buildings in Swarna Bharathi Nagar. We treat the soil foundation before construction begins, creating a chemical barrier that prevents termites from entering the structure for years.

  • Foundation treatment
  • Plinth area treatment
  • External perimeter trenching

2. Post-Construction Treatment (Drill-Fill-Seal)

For existing homes and offices on Hospital Road. We drill small holes at the junction of the wall and floor, inject potent termiticides, and seal them.

  • Wall and floor junction treatment
  • Wooden fixtures treatment
  • Electrical conduit protection
