The Health Risk of Flies

House flies are more than just annoying pests. They are filthy insects that breed in garbage and decaying matter. In a humid place like West Gudur, fly populations can explode quickly, especially near food markets and open drains.

Flies carry over 100 pathogens, including Cholera, Typhoid, Dysentery, and Salmonella. For businesses like restaurants and sweet shops in Gudur, a fly infestation can ruin your reputation and lead to health department fines.

Prevention Tips

  • Cover all food items, especially sweets and fruits.
  • Ensure garbage bins have tight-fitting lids.
  • Install mesh screens on windows and doors.
  • Clean up pet waste immediately.
  • Fix leaking pipes to reduce moisture breeding grounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do UV fly traps work?

UV light attracts flies, and a glue board or electric grid inside traps kills them silently and hygienically.

Is the spray safe for my food business?

Yes, we use food-safe chemicals in sensitive areas and focus on baiting and trapping to avoid contamination.

Can you get rid of fruit flies?

Yes, fruit flies require locating the breeding source (usually fermenting food or drains). We treat the source to stop the cycle.
