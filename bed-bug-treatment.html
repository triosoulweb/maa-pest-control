Signs of Bed Bugs in Your Mattress

  • Red, itchy bites on your body in the morning.
  • Tiny brown or black spots on bedsheets (fecal matter).
  • Sweet, musty odor in the bedroom.

Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to remove with DIY methods. In Gudur's humid climate, they spread quickly. SG Pest Control uses specialized chemical treatments that penetrate mattress crevices and furniture joints.

Our 3-Step Removal Process

1

Inspection

We inspect beds, sofas, curtains, and electrical switchboards.

2

Treatment

Intensive spray treatment of all infested areas. Two rounds are usually required.

3

Follow-up

A second visit after 10-15 days ensures any hatching eggs are destroyed.

Customer Preparation

  • Remove bedding and wash in hot water.
  • Move furniture away from walls if possible.
  • Vacate the room for 3-4 hours during treatment.
